Knorr Lasagne

Knorr Lasagne is probably the best lasagne on the planet. The best part about it?
It's SO simple to make.

Ingredient list

  • 500 g meat
  • 250 ml milk
  • 500 ml water
  • Some cheese

Instructions

  1. Burn the meat in a large pot (enough space for milk, water and powder is needed)
  2. Add milk, water and powder from box
  3. Put into form and put in the oven for 30 minutes at 200 degrees celsius

Go home.
